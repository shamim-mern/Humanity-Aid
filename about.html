What We Do

We campaign with children to remove the barriers that prevent children from surviving, learning and being protected, so that every child can reach their full potential.Humanity campaigns for ultimately the realisation of the all children's rights as set out in the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child, with a focus on children left behind.

We are Team Humanity

A passionate group of people who wishes to inspire a nation and beyond. Humanity is an educational voluntary organization that originated from Bangladesh. The official registration No.

is S-55555/2022). Humanity has been operated by 40 professionals and with help of thousands of volunteers. Our mission is to globally foster philanthropic works. By designing innovative and inspiring programs, our goal is to engage students and professionals to work together to educate, empower and lift the underprivileged. One of our endeavors is to provide free education, educational materials and support.

Where we work

Humanity initially started as an organization serving the poor communities by making learning fun and engaging. Later by crowdfunding and by massive support from the citizens of Bangladesh and International well wishers and donors we have grown into one of the biggest charities in Bangladesh. We have 6 orphanages, schools, libraries, shelters for domestic violence victims and children who are outcast, educate and make them self sufficient. Some of our localised projects are flood relief, educational assistance, free eatery, religious festivals celebrations for the poor, job training etc. We generally work with some of the most vulnerable demographics and specialize in disaster relief works.

Vision and Mission of Humanity

Vision: Our vision is to spread humanity, kindness and bring social justice. We believe that good brings only more good and everyone deserves to live in a peaceful environment.

Mission: Our mission is to provide education, nourishment, mental and physical support to the distressed and to make people self sufficient by permanently rehabilitating them.

Questions / Answers

Which types of work does Humanity do?

Humanity conducts various activities aimed at the basic needs of the people food, clothing, housing, education and medical for survival. In order to meet the basic needs of the deprived children, we have been conducting several regular programs including one taka meal, Iftar and seheri distribution in Ramadan, winter and eid cloth distribution, orphanage, free education program, one taka medical treatment, one glass of milk, ambulance service, training center, scholarship program etc.

How do you manage accounts?

Our accounts are maintained by professional and audited internally besides externally. Every year, ACNABIN, one of the topmost audit firms in Bangladesh audit our financial process. We are the only volunteer organization in Bangladesh, do this external audit.

What type of organization is Humanity?

Humanity is one of the largest volunteer organizations in Bangladesh.

How can my organization support Humanity?

Financial support is not enough to enrich this organization. Rather your expertise in communication to reach more generous peoples for engagement. In following aspects you can associate with us: A) volunteer work B) Train Humanity Team for being more effective C) Donation link for your customers
