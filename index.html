About our humanity

What We Do

We campaign with children to remove the barriers that prevent children from surviving, learning and being protected, so that every child can reach their full potential.Humanity campaigns for ultimately the realisation of the all children's rights as set out in the UN Convention on the Rights of the Child, with a focus on children left behind.

Where we Work

Through our work in Bangladesh, we put the most deprived and marginalised children first, helping them survive, learn and reach their full potential. Find out more about our work across by website.

When you give to Our humanity, you know your donation is making a difference. Whether you are supporting one of our Signature Programs or our carefully curated list of Gifts That Give More, our professional staff works hard every day to ensure every dollar has impact for the cause of your choice.

Years of Experience

We over 320 amazing projects in Bangladesh, and have given over Tk. 300000.00 in cash and product grants to other groups since 2022. We also operate our own dynamic suite of Signature Programs.
